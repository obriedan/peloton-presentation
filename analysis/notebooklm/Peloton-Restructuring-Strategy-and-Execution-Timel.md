**Specific Initiatives & Details**

**Source**

**Feb 2022**

**Plan Announcement**

*   • **Headcount:** Reduce global corporate headcount.<br>• **Manufacturing:** Close several assembly/manufacturing plants; sell the "Peloton Output Park" (POP) shell facility in Ohio.<br>• **Logistics:** Close/consolidate distribution facilities; shift to third-party logistics (3PL) in certain locations.<br>• **Timeline:** Expected to be substantially implemented by the end of FY2024.[[Source 1]](#src-1)

**July 2022**

**Expansion of Scope**

*   • **Manufacturing:** Decision to exit **all** owned-manufacturing operations.<br>• **Partner Strategy:** Expand relationship with Taiwanese manufacturer Rexon Industrial Corp.[[Source 2]](#src-2)

**Aug 2022**

**Additional Actions**

*   • **Logistics:** Eliminate North American Field Ops warehouses; significant reduction of delivery workforce teams.<br>• **Support:** Eliminate significant roles in North America Member Support; exit real estate in Plano and Tempe.<br>• **Retail:** Reduce North American retail showroom presence.[[Source 2]](#src-2)

**Oct 2022**

**Headcount Update**

*   • **Headcount:** Announced elimination of approximately 500 global team member positions.[[Source 3]](#src-3)

**Jan 2024**

**Asset Disposal**

*   • **Real Estate:** Completed the sale of the Peloton Output Park building and a portion of the land for net proceeds of ~$31.9 million.[[Source 4]](#src-4)

**June 2025**

**Final Status**

*   • **Status:** Actions pursuant to the 2022 Restructuring Plan were deemed **substantially completed**. Remaining charges were rolled into subsequent plans.[[Source 5]](#src-5)

# <a id="src-1"></a>Source: annual report 10k 2022.pdf (Citation 1)



## [Main Content](#src-main) 

Restructuring Plan In February 2022, we announced and began implementing a restructuring plan to realign our operational focus to support our multi-year growth, scale the business, and improve costs (the “Restructuring Plan”). The Restructuring Plan includes: (i) reducing our headcount; (ii) closing several assembly and manufacturing plants, including the completion and subsequent sale of the shell facility for our previously planned Peloton Output Park; (iii) closing and consolidating several distribution facilities, and (iv) shifting to third-party logistics providers in certain locations. We expect the Restructuring Plan to be substantially implemented by the end of fiscal 2024.

## [Main Content](#src-main) 

On July 12, 2022, we announced we are exiting all owned-manufacturing operations and our expansion of our current relationship with Taiwanese manufacturer Rexon Industrial Corp. Additionally, on August 12, 2022, we announced our decision to perform the following additional restructuring activities: (i) eliminate our North American Field Ops warehouses, including the significant reduction of our delivery workforce teams; (ii) eliminate a significant number of roles on the North America Member Support team and exit our real-estate footprints in our Plano and Tempe locations; and (iii) reduce our North America retail showroom presence. In connection with the Restructuring Plan, we estimate that we will incur additional cash charges of approximately $95.0 million primarily composed of severance and other exit costs in fiscal year 2023 and beyond. Additionally, we expect to recognize approximately $75.0 million of asset impairment charges in fiscal year 2023 in connection with the Restructuring Plan.

As of June 30, 2025, actions pursuant to our 2024 Restructuring Plan, which includes any remaining restructuring activity under the original 2022 Restructuring Plan, have been substantially completed. As such, any remaining charges under these plans are included within the 2025 Restructuring Plan.

In connection with the 2025 Restructuring Plan, we estimate that we will incur additional cash restructuring charges of approximately $50.0 million, primarily comprised of location strategy costs, lease termination costs, and other exit costs. ## [Main Content](#src-main) 

On April 22, 2024, our Board of Directors approved a new restructuring plan to expand upon its 2022 Restructuring Plan (as expanded, the “2024 Restructuring Plan”). We believe that the 2024 Restructuring Plan will position Peloton for sustained, positive free cash flow, while enabling the Company to continue to invest in software, hardware and content innovation, improvements to its Member support experience, and optimizations to marketing efforts to scale the business. The 2024 Restructuring Plan includes: (i) a reduction in global headcount by approximately 15%, which impacted roughly 400 Peloton team members; and (ii) continued closures of our retail locations. We expect the majority of the 2024 Restructuring Plan to be implemented by the end of fiscal 2025.
