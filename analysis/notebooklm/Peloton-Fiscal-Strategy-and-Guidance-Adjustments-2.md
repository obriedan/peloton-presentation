### 2\. Instances where Management **Lowered** Guidance

Management lowered guidance in only one specific instance within the provided transcripts, related to App subscriptions.

*   **Q1 FY2025 Call (Oct 31, 2024):**
    *   **Metric:** FY2025 Paid App Subscriptions.
    *   **Decrease:** Lowered by **20,000 subscriptions** (New range: 550k–600k).
    *   **Reason:** Management made a strategic decision to limit media spend for the App while investing in product development to improve the member experience before spending to acquire new users[[Source 11]](#src-11).

### 3\. Instances where Management **Declined** or **Withdrew** Guidance

Management explicitly declined to provide specific guidance on certain metrics, citing uncertainty or internal policy.

*   **Future Engagement Benefits (Q1 FY2025 & Q2 FY2025):**
    *   **Declined to Guide:** Management declined to incorporate potential upside from software innovation (such as "Teams" or personalized plans) into their financial guidance.
    *   **Reason:** The timing and magnitude of when these innovations would meaningfully impact engagement and retention were deemed "uncertain"[[Source 11]](#src-11)[[Source 12]](#src-12).
*   **Specific Churn Guidance (Q2 FY2025):**
    *   **Declined to Guide:** When asked for specific churn numbers for the remainder of the year, CFO Liz Coddington stated, "We don't guide specifically to churn," though she provided directional expectations that churn would remain relatively in line with Q2 levels[[Source 13]](#src-13).
*   **Subscription Pricing Plans (Q2 FY2025):**
    *   **Declined to Guide:** When asked how investors should think about potential price increases, CEO Peter Stern declined to provide specifics, stating, "We won't take any actions there lightly. So beyond what I've shared so far, that's about all we have to say today"[[Source 14]](#src-14)[[Source 15]](#src-15).
